Flowchart to find whether a number isprime or composite
1. Start
2. Accept a positive Interger
3. Instalize counter with a value 2
4. divide a number with counter value
5. If the remainder is 0 display that positive number is composite and stop
6. else increment the counter and repeat from step 4 until the counter value is less than n
7. After completion of all steps display that a number 'n' is prime
8. STOP
